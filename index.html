Turmeric Cultivation

Turmeric is a perennial herb, native South Asia. It is also known as "Indian saffron" is sacred spice of India. It is principal ingredient in Indian culinary and used as flavouring and colouring agent. It is used in drug and cosmetic industry because of its anti-cancer and anti- viral property.
